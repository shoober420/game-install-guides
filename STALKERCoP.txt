S.T.A.L.K.E.R.: Call of Pripyat

 - download from steam
 - download latest OpenAL (https://github.com/kcat/openal-soft)
 - place "bin/Win32/soft_aol.dll" in "Stalker Call of Pripyat\bin" directory and rename it OpenAL32.dll (Win64 WONT WORK, crash at launch)


Video settings:
Sun quality: max high
Sun rays: high
SSAO mode: HDAO 
SSAO quality: very high


SSAO = Screen Space Ambient Occlusion
HDAO = High Definition Ambient Occlusion (quality)
HBAO = Horizon-Based Ambient Occlusion (performance)
