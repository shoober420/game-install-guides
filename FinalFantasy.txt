Final Fantasy

- download mesen2 (https://github.com/SourMesen/Mesen2)
- download Final Fantasy JAP rom (Final Fantasy (Japan) (Rev B).nes)
- download Chaos Rush fan translation (https://www.romhacking.net/translations/4868)
- download Lunar IPS (https://www.romhacking.net/utilities/240/)
- extract translation and lunar ips program
- open Lunar IPS
- click "Apply IPS Patch"
- select chaos edition for more accurate translation
- select final fantasy jap rom
- patch complete, launch mesen2
- load patched rom and play
