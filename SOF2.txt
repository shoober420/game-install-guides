Soldier of Fortune 2: Double Helix
 - download sof2 from gog.com or gog galaxy
 - download SuisFix (NOT RECOMMENDED) (https://github.com/suimachine/soldier-of-fortune-2-SuisFix)
  + SuisFix allows clipping on the edges of the screen when up against walls, which allows you to see through walls
  + DONT change resolution via video settings in-game when using SuisFix, breaks desired resolution and brightness

Shift+Tilde to open console
