Bioshock (2007) is better than Bioshock Remastered

VSYNC required to fix strobing and tearing flashing lights

60FPS required to fix cutscene camera angles

Force Global Lighting = On lowers graphics quality, keep Off for HQ lighting

Download OpenAL Soft nightly
 - https://github.com/kcat/openal-soft
 - place Win32 OpenAL32.dll and alsoft.ini into Bioshock.exe directory (Bioshock\Builds\Release)

Use D3D10 and FMOD

config file: %APPDATA%\Bioshock\bioshock.ini

Tweak HavokNumThreads= to amount of threads CPU has

ReduceMouseLag and DoubleBufferMouseLag are BAD and should be set to False

DX9 / RenderDevice=D3DDrv.D3DRenderDevice
DX10 / RenderDevice=D3DDrv10.D3DRenderDevice10

FMOD / AudioDevice=FMODAudio.FMODAudioSubsystem
OpenAL / AudioDevice=ALAudio.ALAudioSubsystem

bEAXEnabled=True + AudioDevice=FMODAudio.FMODAudioSubsystem fixes NO SOUND after new game cutscene

Add MouseSmoothingMode=0 under [Engine.PlayerInput] in User.ini



add "-dx10" launch option
