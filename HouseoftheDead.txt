House of the Dead

- download Model 2 Emulator (https://segaretro.org/Model_2_Emulator)
- download House of the Dead (google model 2 emulator ROM sets) (mame roms and model 2 roms are different)
- extract M2Emulator
- create games directory inside M2Emulator directory
- open EMULATOR.INI
- add "%USERPROFILE%\Downloads\M2emulator_1.1a\games\" to Dir1=
