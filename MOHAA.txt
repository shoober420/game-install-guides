Medal of Honor: Allied Assault Warchest
 - download from gog or gog galaxy
 - download unofficial patch (https://www.moddb.com/games/medal-of-honor-allied-assault/addons/medal-of-honor-allied-assault-unofficial-patch-10)
 - extract into "Medal of Honor - Allied Assault War Chest\main" directory

- download openmohaa (https://github.com/openmoh/openmohaa)

r_sse 1 MIGHT crash game at loading screen

r_maxpolys tweaked too high MIGHT crash the game

r_ext_compress_textures 1 ENABLES HQ TEXTURES

TWEAK r_customwidth AND r_customheight BEFORE LAUNCH

OpenMoHAA config dir is "%USERPROFILE%/AppData/Roaming/openmohaa"
