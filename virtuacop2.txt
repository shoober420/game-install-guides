Virtua Cop 2
 - download (https://www.myabandonware.com/game/virtua-cop-2-byi)
 - install by mounting image and extracting VCOP2 directory to "C:\SEGA"

dgvoodoo2
 - download dgvoodoo2
 - extract dgVoodooCpl.exe and the contents of the MS\x86 subfolder to "C:\SEGA\VCOP2\PROJECT"
 - General tab, Output API: Direct3D 12 (feature level 12.0), Adapter(s) to use / enable: SELECT MAIN VIDEO CARD (All of them doesnt work)
 - DirectX tab, VRAM: 4096 MB, Filtering: Force anisotropic 16x, Resolution: 1920x1080, Antialiasing (MSAA): Off, dgvoodoo Watermark: checkmark

Device Select (F5)
Provider (F9) (for multiplayer)
Quit (ALT+F4)

F10 to enable menu at top of screen

Device
 - Direct 3D (display)*** (important to enable)

Settings
 - Display Settings (F4)*** (important to tweak)
 - Mode Settings (F6)
 - Device Settings (F7)
 - Skip Frames (disable)
 - Shading (enable)
