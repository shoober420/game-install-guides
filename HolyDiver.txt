Holy Diver

- download mesen2 (https://github.com/SourMesen/Mesen2)
- download Holy Diver JAP rom
- download fan translation (https://www.romhacking.net/translations/1110/)
- download Lunar IPS (https://www.romhacking.net/utilities/240/)
- extract translation and lunar ips program
- open Lunar IPS
- click "Apply IPS Patch"
- select mfc_holydiver_eng10.ips
- select holy diver jap rom
- patch complete, launch mesen2
- load patched rom and play
